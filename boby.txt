> **First Time Installation:**

- [ ] Format data `if encrypted`
- [ ] Reboot to recovery
- [ ] Back up your efs, persist, modem partitions `5x baseband`
- [ ] Wipe system, vendor, cache, dalvik, data
- [ ] Flash ROM zip and magisk `optional`
- [ ] Flash [A1-V10.0.24.0_Pie_Baseband.zip](https://t.me/TiffanyUpdates/523?single) `First Time`
- [ ] Reboot & Enjoy

> **Notes:**

- [ ] To restore the 5X baseband, please use twrp to restore the efs, persist, modem backup files.
